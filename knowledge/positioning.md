## Geoffrey Moore Positioning Statement

**For** enterprise security and IAM teams
**Who** struggle with fragmented identity tools, visibility gaps, and an expanding identity attack surface
**Linx Security is** the next-generation identity security and governance platform
**That** eliminates blind spots by ingesting and organizing identity data across all systems, providing contextualized insights, and automating workflows to continuously reduce the identity attack surface
**Unlike** traditional IGA tools (SailPoint, Saviynt) that focus on compliance workflows, or point ISPM tools that surface risk but can't act on it
**Linx** bridges visibility, intelligence, and action in a single platform — delivering measurable attack surface reduction, not just certification campaigns.

## "Only We" Claim (Draft)

"Only Linx combines deep identity data ingestion with contextualized posture insights AND automated governance workflows — so you can see your entire identity attack surface, understand what's risky, and fix it, without stitching together 3 different tools."

**Stress test this against:**
- Veza: Also does visibility + intelligence, but weaker on IGA action layer (pre-ServiceNow)
- ConductorOne: Strong on action (IGA), weaker on visibility/ISPM
- Lumos: Building the full loop via agents, but less proven in enterprise
